Play

Lexicon Standoff

Starting a Game

A Player (Host) starts a game on a phone, tablet, or computer by visiting lexiconstandoff.com. The Host shares the gamesecret with other Players, who join the game on their phone, tablet, or computer. Once all Players are in the lobby, the Host starts the first Round.

Gameplay

Players are assigned a role of Agent or Imposter at the beginning of each Round. Role Distribution is as close to 3 Agents to 1 Imposter as possible with at least 1 Imposter.

A Round is split into four stages:

  • Selections: Players take selections saying a word for two given Topics. Agents have better intel than Imposters.
  • Reveal: The two Topics are revealed to all Players.
  • Actions: Players vote who to kill.
  • Results: Players are killed, scores are displayed. Rinse and Repeat.

Selections

There are two Topics generated for the Round (eg. “Goose” & “Spider”). Agents will only see the two Topics on their selection. Imposters will see both in addition to one extra. They will not know which are the real Topics and which are fake.

  • Agents will see “Goose” & “Spider”
  • Imposters will see “Goose”, “Clowns”, “Spider”

Players submit one word they associate with both Topics.

  • A good Agent word for “Goose” & “Spider” would be “Web” since geese have webbed feet and spiders make webs.
  • A good Imposter word for “Goose”, “Clowns”, “Spider” would be “Fear” since geese, spiders, and clowns are all phobias.

After every Player submits their word, the Game moves to the Reveal stage.

Reveal

All Players will see the real two Topics on their device in addition to every Player's choice. They can ask other Players why they chose their word.

Agents are trying to figure out who are Imposters and the Imposters are trying to turn Agents against each other. Everyone is trying to appear confident in their decision. Players may give any explanation as to why they chose their word, even if it is a lie. This is not about who chose the best word.

Actions

Every Player votes on their device for who they want to kill. After every Player submits their action, the Round will progress on its own.

Results

However many Imposters there are in the game, at least that many Players will be killed. If there is a tie for the most votes (or second most if required), all Players receiving that many votes will be killed.

  • Imposters are revealed to all Players.
  • Game Standings will be displayed
  • The Host can start a new Round which will assign new roles for each Player, or they can end the Game.

Scoring

Surviving Imposters receive two points and surviving Agents receive one point.
